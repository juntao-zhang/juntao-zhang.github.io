I am a Ph.D. student in Mechanical Engineering at Iowa State University.

My research focuses on AI-powered Atomic Force Microscopy (AFM), with an emphasis on image analysis, artifact detection, and physics-aware reconstruction.

Specifically, I work on:

- AFM image restoration and artifact removal  
- Smart flattening algorithms for surface correction  
- Deep learning-based segmentation and detection  
- AFM digital twin simulation and tip–sample interaction modeling  

I develop end-to-end intelligent pipelines that integrate computer vision, machine learning, and physical modeling to improve the accuracy and reliability of AFM measurements.

---

### Current Work

- Developing a lightweight segmentation model for AFM artifact detection  
- Building an interactive AFM repair GUI with automated pipelines  
- Designing a digital twin framework for AFM scanning simulation  
- Exploring inverse estimation of probe geometry
